Hello my name is Jenna Sasaki and I am a first year Business Administration Major at the University of California Riverside. In the future, I plan on concentrating in Management, in hopes to pursue the field of Human Resource Management. While I still want to research more of the other concentrations UC Riverside has to offer, management has always been one I am interested in.

I was born and raised in San Diego, California alongside my older sister, twin brother, and parents. Growing up, I attended weekly Sunday services at the Buddhist Temple of San Diego with my family and relatives. I played many sports growing up and excelled in Track in Field during my four years of high school. Some other extracurricular activities I participated in included Save the Earth Club, Link Crew, Fight Against Hunger, and Humanitarian Outreach Club. While balancing these activities, I worked as a shift leader at a local bubble tea franchise: Kung Fu Tea located in Carmel Mountain Ranch. During my senior year of high school, I really got a feel for how to allocate my time and plan accordingly to different commitments.

At UC Riverside, I am involved in the UC Riverside Nikkei Student Union, where Japanese American culture can be shared with allies of the community. Next year, I will be serving on the general board as the Social Chair, planning icebreakers, games, socials, retreats, and more! I also plan on becoming more involved in the Undergraduate Business Association, learning more about business strategies and career opportunities. Next year, I am planning on working as a Resident Housing Ambassador in the Resident Service Offices here at UCR. In this position, I will be serving my peers by distributing packages, sorting mail, providing resources on campus, and working with other Residential Staff. Being involved in the on-campus community is so important to me, as it helps me become more close-knit with the residents and students on campus

With work experience gained over the past few years, I got the opportunity to learn what it is like working in a fast paced environment. In this type of atmosphere, being able to provide quality service efficiently is key. Customer service has always been a strength of mine, as I love to work and communicate with other people. I have always been a very friendly and social person, loving to meet new people and make friends. Implementing this skill into Human Resources, I hope to help foster a welcoming, safe, and most importantly efficient workplaces where all employees of any race, religion, culture, sexual orientation, and background can perform their best.
